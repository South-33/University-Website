Events & Activities

There's always something happening on campus. Here are some of our signature events.

Cultural Festival

An annual celebration of the diverse cultures within our university community, featuring food, music, and dance.

Welcome Week

A week full of events, fairs, and mixers to help new students connect and find their way around campus.

Annual Hackathon

A 48-hour challenge where students collaborate to build innovative solutions to real-world problems.
