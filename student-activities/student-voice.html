Watch Our Students talk about the Effects of Electronic Cigarettes

How serious are the effects of electronic cigarettes really?

This is an educational video produced by students majoring in Economics at the National University of Management, focusing on the impacts of electronic cigarettes.

The video aims to raise awareness about the harmful effects of electronic cigarettes, which are often perceived as a safer alternative to traditional smoking.
