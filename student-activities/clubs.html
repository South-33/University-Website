Why Join a Club?

Being part of a student club is one of the best ways to enrich your university experience. It's an opportunity to develop valuable leadership skills, build lasting friendships, and become more involved in campus life. Clubs also offer a platform for career development, helping you network with peers and professionals in your field of interest.

Have an Idea for a New Club?

If you can't find a club that matches your interests, we encourage you to start your own! Founding a club is a fantastic way to build leadership skills and create a new community on campus.

Steps to start a new club:

  1. Gather a group of at least 5 interested students.
  2. Find a faculty or staff advisor.
  3. Draft a club constitution outlining your mission and goals.
  4. Submit your new club application to the Student Activities Office.
