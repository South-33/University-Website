Key Centers and Institutions

Center of Excellence for R&D and Innovation (CERI)

The National University of Management’s Center of Excellence for R&D and Innovation (CERI), previously NUM-Research Center, was established in 2013. It emerged from the university’s initiative to bridge academia and the business community, fostering collaboration and promoting research activities among faculty members and researchers worldwide.

ASEAN Economic Research Institution (AERI)

Chosen by the Ministry of Education, Youth, and Sports, this institute supports academic research on economic issues and policy development, aligning with the goals of the ASEAN Economic Community (AEC). It confirms NUM's commitment to enhance and encourage research in economic strategy and policy, driven by Cambodia's ASEAN chairmanship.

Center for Digital Law

Located at the NUM Veal Sbov Campus, this center collaborates with various faculties to support technology transfer, conduct research on technology and digital laws, and provide specialized legal training in the digital domain.

The CamboVerse Center

This center focuses on developing virtual and augmented reality experiences to preserve and disseminate Cambodian cultural heritage. By merging modern technology with cultural education, it aims to foster a deeper global appreciation of Cambodian culture and history.

Administration and Student Services

Located at the NUM Veal Sbov Campus, this section provides essential administrative support, streamlines student enrollment, monitors academic progress, and offers comprehensive guidance and counseling to enhance the overall student experience and support their holistic development.

NUM Social Innovation Lab

Part of the EU-funded South-East Asian Social Innovation Network (SEASIN) and supported by SMART Axiata, this lab is a space for seminars, workshops, and an incubator to develop and launch new social ventures and promote social entrepreneurship.

NUM Career Center

Supported by USAID, this center is part of the "Digital Career Development" project with UC Berkeley. It aims to enhance digital employment skills, increase scholarships and internships, and build relationship opportunities to support the careers of Cambodian youth.

Digital Technology Center (DTC)

Donated by the Ministry of Post and Telecommunication, the DTC is dedicated to developing a university research hub, corporate innovation facilities, and a community tech hub. It provides essential infrastructure, training, and support to advance digital skills, technologies, and transformation.
