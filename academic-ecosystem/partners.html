Our Vision for Global Partnership

The National University of Management (NUM) believes that partnerships are essential to realizing our long-term vision to become a leading research university in advancing entrepreneurship and innovation.

In that sense, NUM has been working effectively to co-operate with domestic and international higher educational institutions, corporations, and organizations. We always look forward to exchanging knowledge and human resources with public and private entities in order to achieve outstanding results together.
