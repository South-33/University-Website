What is Student Mobility?

Student mobility is your opportunity to gain international experience during your studies. It allows you to study, intern, or conduct research abroad for a semester, a year, or even a few weeks. These experiences are designed to enrich your academic journey and enhance your future employability.

"My semester abroad was the single most important experience of my university life. It opened my eyes to new cultures and gave me the confidence to pursue a global career."

- A Former Exchange Student

Key Advantages

  • Develop Cross-Cultural Skills: Immerse yourself in a new environment and learn to navigate different social and professional landscapes.
  • Build a Global Career: Gain a competitive edge with international work or study experience that stands out to employers.
  • Enrich Your Academic Journey: Take specialized courses not available at NUM and bring a new perspective back to your studies.

Your Journey, Step-by-Step

Phase 1: Exploration

September - October

Research & Information Sessions

Explore partner universities using our search tool and attend mandatory info sessions to understand the opportunities and requirements.

Phase 2: Application

November 15th - Deadline

Prepare & Submit Application

Gather your documents (transcripts, motivation letter, recommendations) and submit your complete application through the online portal before the deadline.

Phase 3: Selection

December - January

Nomination & Acceptance

Successful candidates are nominated to partner universities. You will receive an official acceptance letter from your host institution.

Phase 4: Preparation

February - April

Pre-Departure Orientation

Attend pre-departure workshops covering visas, accommodation, insurance, and cultural adaptation. Finalize your learning agreement and travel plans.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to apply?

Most programs are open to full-time undergraduate and graduate students who have completed at least one year of study and meet the minimum GPA requirement.

Are there scholarships available?

Yes, the university offers several mobility scholarships. Additionally, many host institutions and external organizations provide funding opportunities. Visit our Financial Support page for details.

How do course credits get transferred?

Before you go, you will complete a learning agreement to get your courses pre-approved by your academic advisor. This ensures a smooth credit transfer process upon your return.
