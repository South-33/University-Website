About Our University

About NUM

The National University of Management, located in Cambodia, is a modern educational institution with a history of 42 years. NUM has progressed in capacity, diversity, and quality education, emphasizing research, entrepreneurship, and innovation.

Ranked 69th in the World University Rankings for Innovation (WURI) 2025, NUM is committed to developing competent, socially responsible individuals who contribute to Cambodia's socio-economic growth.

Established for public benefit and recognized nationally, NUM has a proud history of offering access to a wide range of academic opportunities.
