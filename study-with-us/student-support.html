Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our support services

How do I reset my student portal password?

You can reset your password by visiting the student portal login page and clicking "Forgot Password?". You'll receive a reset link via email. If you need additional help, contact our Technical Support team at (555) 555-TECH or visit us in the IT Help Desk on the first floor of the library.

Where can I get help with stress and anxiety?

Our Counseling & Mental Health services offer confidential support for stress, anxiety, and other mental health concerns. You can schedule an appointment online through the student portal, call (555) 123-HELP, or visit the Health & Wellness Center. All services are completely free and confidential for students.

How do I find a tutor for my classes?

The Academic Support Center manages all tutoring services. Log into the student portal and navigate to "Academic Resources" to view available tutors, schedules, and book sessions. We offer both individual and group tutoring for most subjects, and all services are free for students.

What financial aid resources are available?

Our Financial Aid office helps with FAFSA applications, scholarship searches, emergency grants, and budget planning. We also offer workshops on financial literacy and managing student debt. Visit us in the Student Services building or schedule an appointment online.

How do I access career counseling services?

Career Services offers resume reviews, interview practice, job search assistance, and career planning. Book an appointment through the student portal under "Career Services" or visit us on the second floor of the Student Union. We also host regular career fairs and networking events.

Are these services really free for all students?

Yes! All student support services are completely free and included in your student fees. This includes tutoring, counseling, career services, technical support, and administrative assistance. Our goal is to ensure every student has access to the resources they need to succeed.
