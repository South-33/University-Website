Overview of Fees

Your university fees are an investment in a world-class education. They cover all essential academic and administrative services, including tuition for your courses, registration processing, and access to our state-of-the-art library, computer labs, and other student support facilities.

Fee Breakdown (Per Semester)

All fees are listed in USD and are subject to change.

Fee Category Domestic Rate International Rate Notes
Tuition Fee $1,500 $2,500 Based on a standard 15-credit load.
Registration Fee $100 $150 One-time fee per semester.
Lab Fee $75 $75 Applicable to Science & Engineering students only.
Library Fee $50 $50 Includes digital and physical resource access.
Student Services Fee $125 $125 Covers career services, wellness, etc.
