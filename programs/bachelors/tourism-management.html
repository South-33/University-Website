Program Curriculum

Year 1

Foundations in Tourism

Semester I

  • Community-Based Tourism (CBT)
  • Introduction to Tourism (ITT)
  • Principle of Tourism Management (PTM)
  • Communication Skill in Tourism Business (CTB)
  • Tourism, Culture, and Heritage (TCH)

Semester II

  • English for Tourism and Hosp. 1 (ET1)
  • Soft Skills in Hospitality (SSH)
  • E-Tourism (ETO)
  • Global Tourism and Hospitality (GTH)
  • Tourism Geography and Environment (TGE)

Year 2

Core Tourism and Business

Semester I

  • Accounting Principle (Tourism) (POA)
  • The Business of Tourism (BTH)
  • Principle of Economics (POE)
  • Business Statistic (BUS)
  • English for Tourism and Hosp. 2 (ET2)

Semester II

  • Planning Research in Hosp. & Tourism (MAR)
  • Tourism Economics (TOE)
  • Tourism Regulation (TRG)
  • Financial Management (Tourism) (FM)
  • English for Tourism and Hosp. 2 (ET2)

Year 3

Advanced Tourism Operations

Semester I

  • Front Office Management (FFO)
  • Ticketing and Tour. Transports Mgt. (TTM)
  • Tourism Service Quality (TSQ)
  • Tourism Marketing (TKM)
  • Consumer Behavior (Tourism) (CBT)

Semester II

  • Travel and Tour Operation (TTO)
  • Food and Beverage Management (FBM)
  • Sustainable Tourism Development (STD)
  • MICE Management (MIM)
  • Human Resources Mgt. (Tourism) (HRM)

Year 4

Specialization and Capstone

Semester I

  • Research Methodology (Tourism) (REM)
  • Travel Agency Management (TAM)
  • Hotel Management (HTM)
  • Tourism Planning & Development (TPD)
  • Tourist Guide (TOG)

Semester II

  • Customer Relation. Mgt. in Tourism (CRM)
  • Tourism Events Management (TEM)
  • Entrepreneurship in Tourism (ETT)
  • Tourism Attraction Management (TAM')
  • Strategic Management in Tourism (STM)

Turn your passion for travel and service into a dynamic career across the tourism and hospitality industry.

CLIENT SERVICE

MARKETING
