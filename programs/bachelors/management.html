Program Curriculum

Year 1

Foundational Business Skills

Semester I

  • Introduction to Business (ITB)
  • Public Administration (PUA)
  • Cambodian Economy (CAE)
  • Principles of Management (PMG)
  • Business English 1 (BUE1)

Semester II

  • Business Law (BUL)
  • Basics of Computer (BAC)
  • Mathematical Analysis (MAT)
  • Principles of Marketing (PMK)
  • Business English 2 (BUE2)

Year 2

Core Management and Analytical Skills

Semester I

  • Research Methodology (REM)
  • Organizational Behavior (ORB)
  • Business Statistics (BUS)
  • Microeconomics (ME)
  • Writing Skills (WRS)

Semester II

  • Marketing for Services (MAS)
  • Human Resource Management (HRM)
  • Principles of Accounting (POA)
  • Macroeconomics (MAE)
  • Business Correspondence (BUC)

Year 3

Advanced Management Applications

Semester I

  • Managerial Accounting (MAA)
  • Environmental Business Management (EBM)
  • Cross Cultural Management (CCM)
  • Marketing Research (MRE)
  • Performance Management (PEM)

Semester II

  • Office Management (OFM)
  • Financial Management (FMG)
  • Marketing Management (MAM)
  • E-Commerce (E-com)
  • Inventory Management (INM)

Year 4

Strategic Leadership and Capstone

Semester I

  • Management of Technology (MOT)
  • Investment Management (IVM)
  • Project Management (PRM)
  • Total Quality Management (TQM)
  • Commercial Transactions (COT)

Semester II

  • Business Ethics (BET)
  • Entrepreneurship (ENS)
  • Logistic Management (LOG)
  • International Business Management (IBM)
  • Strategic Management (STM)
  • Research Writing & Presentation (RWP)
