Program Overview

An intensive MBA designed for early-to-mid career professionals seeking executive leadership roles. Build strategic, analytical, and people leadership capabilities through a rigorous, practice-oriented curriculum.

Duration
18–24 months
Format
Full-time | Part-time
Intakes
Fall | Spring
Credits
~60 ECTS (or equivalent)

Curriculum Snapshot

Core Courses

  • Strategic Leadership
  • Advanced Organizational Behavior
  • Corporate Finance for Managers
  • Operations Strategy
  • Marketing Strategy in the Digital Age
  • Business Analytics for Decision-Making

Experiential & Capstone

  • Consulting Project with Industry Partner
  • Innovation Lab Practicum (NUM Social Innovation Lab)
  • Global Study Visit / Exchange (subject to availability)
  • Leadership & Communication Workshops
