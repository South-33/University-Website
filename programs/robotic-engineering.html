Bachelor of Science in Robotic Engineering

Build the future of automation and artificial intelligence with our hands-on engineering program.

Program Objectives

  • 1.Understand core concepts and develop professional and theoretical skills in Robotics and AI.
  • 2.Apply machine learning and integrate software with hardware using AI techniques to enhance robotic capabilities.
  • 3.Build a mindset to apply practical orientation needed to link up the Robotics and AI sector with the digital government and industry.
  • 4.Provide most of the Robotics and AI professionals needed by the Cambodia and sub-region countries.
  • 5.Work in multidisciplinary teams and promote ethical and sustainable practices among professionals.
