WELCOME TO NUM

For over 40 years, the National University of Management has progressively developed its capacity, diversity, and quality of education. We are guided by our core values of research, entrepreneurship, and innovation, which pave the way for a brighter future for our students and alumni.

OUR MISSION

NUM is firmly committed to developing competent and socially responsible human resources. We equip our graduates with high intellectual knowledge, practical skills, and strong ethics, enabling them to make productive contributions to the socio-economic development of the country.

OUR VISION

To be the leading research university in Cambodia for entrepreneurship and innovation. Our strategic plan expresses a strong commitment to transform NUM by applying the highest quality standards in education, research, and community engagement.
